Arcade Girl (ver. 0.3.0)

  • Avoid enemies and go to the water!
  • Use arrow keys to control the player.
  • New characters will be unlocked after clearing level 1, 2, 3, and 5.
  • Press A to open character-introduction panel, press LEFT or RIGHT to surf, and press X to close. Grey characters have not been locked.
  • Press C to change your current character.
  • Press P to pause, and then press any other key to resume.
